Who is Christian?

My name is Christian, I was born in 1993 and I am living in Denmark. Currently I study at Aalborg university which is fuelling my passion for every type of digital media. This includes both the highly interactive kind (video games, web sites, social media, ect.), the less interactive kind (movies, YouTube videos, ect.), communication through those types of media, face-to-face interactions and a lot of things in between. To explain all that I have learned in this segment would take too long. That is why I created this page.
Besides my studies I like to play video games (I own to different home consoles), I enjoy watching movies, I really like to play Dungeons & Dragons (yes I admit it so stop laughing) and I also use my time as a member of Rotaract.

Me as a person

I am driven by learning new things and at the same time getting better at what I do.

University experince

I have studied Communication and Digital Media (now specializing in Interactive Digital Media) at Aalborg University from September of 2017.
Throughout these years I have acquired knowledge of and experience on how to create good research design, critical thinking, collaboration skills in big and small groups, working with audio and video recording and editing, basic coding skills in HTML and CSS, researching topics in detail and self-disciple.
I have accumulated a great amount of knowledge regarding mediated communication, how interactive media works, how people react in different mediated situation, what instruments and means influences individuals to act and react in certain ways when interacting with different types of media.
